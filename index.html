What is the 75-Day CS Roadmap?

The 75-Day CS Roadmap: A structured, results-driven program for Math and CS students, offering a step-by-step guide to mastering foundational and advanced Computer Science concepts. It emphasizes technical skills, problem-solving, and confidence building, equipping you to kickstart your tech journey effectively. 🚀

The 75-Day Journey

Day 1-25

Basics of Programming and Problem Solving with specializations in C, C++, Java, DotNet, Python, C#.

Day 26-50

Industry-Oriented Skills (Web Development, Mobile App Development, Database Management, Testing Methodologies, Cloud Platforms).

Day 51-75

Personal Growth and Career Guidance (Networking, Projects, Interview Preparation, Internship opportunities with certification).
